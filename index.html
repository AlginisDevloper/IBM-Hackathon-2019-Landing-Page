Hackathon Details

IBM is announcing IBM Digital–Nation Competition in collaboration with ITI (MCIT’s technical delivery arm). The competition addresses Egypt pollution issues (air, water, noise, light, soil, etc.) and the solutions that can be proposed by Egyptian youth to deal with escalating pollution crisis.

IBM aims to leverage its latest MEA academic initiative: Digital–Nation Africa (D-NA), and latest emerging technologies via IBM Cloud to help developers learn, innovate, and build solutions to help Egypt reduce pollution and prevent disasters like drought, floods, and massive fatal diseases like cancer, strokes, and hypertension.
