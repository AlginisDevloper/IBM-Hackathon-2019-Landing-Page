Competition Details

IBM is announcing IBM Digital – Nation Competition in collaboration with Information Technology Institute (ITI) and the American Chamber of Commerce (AmCham). The competition addresses Egypt pollution issues (air, water, noise, light, soil, etc...) and the solutions that can be proposed by Egyptian youth to deal with escalating pollution crisis. Emerging Technologies such as AI, IoT, Cloud, Analytics, and Blockchain, coupled by smart innovative IT developers and research, can help reduce pollution.

IBM aims to leverage its latest MEA academic initiative IBM Digital - Nation Africa , and latest emerging technologies via IBM Cloud to help developers learn, innovate, and build solutions to help Egypt reduce pollution and prevent disasters like drought, floods, and massive fatal diseases like cancer, strokes, and hypertension.
